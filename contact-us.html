How to Contact Us

We love to receive feedback and hear from those who use our service. We want to keep getting better and we couldn't think of a better way than to hear from you! If you wanted to just send us a thank you message too, by all means, it would make our day.

If you did have a question, please check out the Q&A below before you send us a message. We want to make sure you get what you need to know as quickly as possible. If you don't see a satisfying answer, we'd love to help!

Frquently Asked Questions

Q: Do I need to pay for any of the content?

A: No! That's the exciting thing about this project that we've been working on. We want you to have every opportunity to get started and get better at playing guitar so we've decided to make everything free!


Q: How often should I practice?

A: If you want to have the best chance to improve you should practice every day, but that doesn't mean for hours at a time. Even 15 minutes will do, just make sure to make it a habit. As for the guides, only move on when you feel you've gotten a very strong grasp on the concepts at hand.


Q: When will there be new content?

A: We are hoping to add new guides in a timely manner, but we are only doing this on the side and when we have time, unfortunately. We need to pay the bills somehow, afterall.
