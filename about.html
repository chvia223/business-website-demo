About Christian's Guitar

We, here at Christian's Guitar, believe that each person with the desire to learn the guitar should have the experience of doing so with confidence and consistency. That is why we are providing a streamlined guide of the things that have helped us improve over the years. The goal in doing this is to make powerful and productive learning material that is as accessible to as many people as possible. The principles that we will teach, pulled from current popular music, will provide confidence and relevance to those who want to show off their new skills. Expect to stretch and grow as you explore the guitar.

We are a small team of amatuer players seeking to provide guidance to those exploring their craft in guitar. We formed our group after we met with similar passions in college. Since then we've been working hard to form what we hope will be valuable resources to you on your journey.
